Where you can grow

Production & logistics

The heart of the company. Modern production lines, packing and dispatch of millions of units a year — with an emphasis on precision and safety.

Quality & laboratory

Testing raw materials, batches and finished products. Work for meticulous people who know the details matter.

Product development

New formulas, forms and brands. From the first idea to a product on the drugstore shelf or in a racer's backpack.

Sales & export

From Czech retail chains to dozens of foreign markets. A sales team that opens doors for our brands and partners.

Marketing & digital

Brands, e-commerce, content and AI tools. The group's digital team builds what millions of customers will see.

Back office & support

Finance, HR, IT and administration — the teams without which none of it would happen.
