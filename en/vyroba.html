Cooperation models

Three routes to your product

Whether you have just an idea, a finished formula, or you're looking for a proven product for your brand — we have a cooperation model for you.

Custom development

From idea to formula. Our development team designs the composition, form and dosage to your brief and target group — including samples and stability testing.

Private label

Proven formulas from our portfolio with your label and packaging. The fastest route to market — no development costs, with flexible batch volumes.

Contract manufacturing (OEM)

Have your own formula? We'll manufacture it to your documentation — in proven quality, with full batch traceability and reliable lead times.

Forms & technologies

A form to suit your customer

We produce the most common food supplement forms and keep expanding our technological capabilities.

  • Tablets and effervescent tablets — the classic, with precise dosing.
  • Capsules and blisters — a premium format for more demanding lines.
  • Sachets and stick packs — practical single-serve doses.
  • Syrups and liquid forms — favourites for children's and family products.
  • Jellies and gummies — a modern form that kids and adults love.

From effervescent tablets to gummies shaped like the five-petal flower.

How the cooperation works

From enquiry to dispatch in four steps

1 · Enquiry & consultation

You describe your plan — product, target group, volume and market. Within a few days we get back to you with a recommendation and a first proposal.

2 · Formula & samples

We prepare the formula and samples for your approval. With private label you choose from ready-made formulas — it's faster.

3 · Documentation & compliance

We help with labels, notifications and legislation for the Czech, Slovak and other European markets.

4 · Production & dispatch

We manufacture, pack and ship. Every batch comes with a certificate of analysis and complete documentation.

Quality & trust

Quality you can prove

Your brand is only as strong as your manufacturer. That's why you get full transparency.

  • In-house laboratory — we test incoming raw materials and finished products ourselves.
  • Certificate of analysis (CoA) — with every batch produced.
  • Batch traceability — from raw material to the dispatched box.
  • Quality management system — production according to HACCP principles and good manufacturing practice.

Quality control in our own laboratory in Zlín.
