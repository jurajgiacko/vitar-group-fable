Our story

A family business from Zlín, a European group

We started in 1990 with a simple idea: vitamins you can trust. Today, VITAR Group is a group of brands, manufacturing and people, helping customers in dozens of markets live a healthier life.

The brand that connects

VITAR Group is the umbrella identity of the whole group. It doesn't sell — it identifies, connects and builds trust. Behind it stand concrete brands, a manufacturing base and, above all, the people who give it all meaning.

We have carried the five-petal flower in our logo from the very beginning. It is a symbol of luck, nature and the five things we build on: quality, science, family, honesty and the joy of a healthy life.

One symbol, one whole group.

Milestones

More than three decades of growth

  1. 1990

    The family business VITAR is founded in Zlín. Shortly after the revolution, we begin making our first vitamin products for the Czech market.

  2. 1990s

    The portfolio grows with classics the whole country still knows today — effervescent vitamins, Vyproštovák and Energit lozenges.

  3. 2000+

    The MaxiVita brand becomes a fixture of drugstores and retail. We expand production, the laboratory and R&D, and start exporting on a larger scale.

  4. 2010+

    The sports world joins the group — distribution of Italian Enervit nutrition and the Czech compression brand Royal Bay. Our products head to dozens of foreign markets.

  5. 2026

    The group steps out under a new umbrella identity, VITAR Group. One roof for brands, manufacturing, e-commerce and careers.

Responsibility

We share our success

Our motto — helping people live a healthier life — applies beyond production too.

We help

A Dobrý anděl (Good Angel) donor, a partner of the Jedu na dřeň bone-marrow registry project since 2016 and of the NEO CUP charity for prematurely born children. We support cultural projects and have adopted animals in the Zlín and Brno zoos.

We collaborate

With the TiRO Blansko and bika sheltered workshops, which take part in packing our products, with the Faculty of Multimedia Communications at Tomas Bata University in Zlín, and with the Czech Chamber of Commerce.

Sustainability

We publish a non-financial report voluntarily, ahead of the CSRD obligation — partners, employees and communities deserve structured information about our progress.
