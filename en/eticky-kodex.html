Compliance

Code of ethics

Our employee's word is VITAR's word. In everything we do, we follow the basic rules of fair conduct, agreed terms and applicable law.

Customers

  • Honesty and fairness towards customers are the foundation of a lasting business relationship.
  • Corrupt practices of any kind are unacceptable.
  • Customer information is treated as confidential.

Suppliers

  • We buy inputs and services solely through legitimate business methods and never abuse our market position.
  • We honour all contractual arrangements and payment discipline.

Employees

  • Relationships are built on respect for the dignity of every person.
  • We hire and promote based on competence for the job — without discrimination of any kind.
  • We provide a safe working environment and room for professional growth; no form of harassment is tolerated.

Shareholders

  • Annual reports are truthful, accurate and on time.
  • Shareholders are regularly informed about business policy, results and outlook.

State, region and society

  • We keep proper accounts and meet our obligations to public authorities on time.
  • We financially support sport and culture and run an active charitable policy.

Environment

  • We continually work to improve the environment and adjust our processes to minimise the impact of production.

Competitors

  • We compete vigorously but fairly. We do not damage competitors’ reputations or obtain information dishonestly.

The full text of the VITAR, s.r.o. Code of Ethics is available on request at vitar@vitar.cz.
