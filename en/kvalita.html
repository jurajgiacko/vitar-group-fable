Quality & certification

What we declare on the label must be inside

For the whole shelf life of the product. We don't delegate quality — we develop, manufacture and test in-house, and back it with international certifications.

Certification

The standards we vouch for

As a manufacturer distributing worldwide, we voluntarily undergo internationally recognised food-production certifications.

ISO 22000

The international food safety management system — the certified standard our entire supplement production runs on.

FSSC 22000

Food Safety System Certification — a GFSI-recognised standard that retail chains accept as proof of food safety on the manufacturer's side.

Czech Quality Charter

A signatory of the Czech Quality Charter since 2016 — a commitment to promote the good name of Czech quality at home and abroad.

How we control

Multi-level control, internal and external

  • Incoming material control — every raw material and primary packaging passes quality control; suppliers are regularly evaluated and randomly audited.
  • Notification and analysis — before launch, every product is notified to the Czech Ministry of Agriculture and passes microbial and chemical analysis in an accredited laboratory.
  • State oversight — compliance is inspected by the Czech Agriculture and Food Inspection Authority; around 10 inspections a year take place at VITAR.
  • Certificate of Analysis (CoA) — with every batch produced, fully traceable from raw material to dispatch.

Declared active substances are verified to internal rules.

Legislation and honesty

Food supplements under scrutiny

Food supplements are food — all food legislation applies, including EFSA rules for health and nutrition claims. The label carries the complete composition, and every statement must be true.

“Every raw material and primary packaging has defined incoming quality controls. We also run random internal audits at our suppliers and follow a clearly defined system for selecting raw materials,” says Jana Pszczolková, Director of Quality Control, Development and Legislation.
